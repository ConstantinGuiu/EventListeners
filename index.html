Explanations

There are 7 EventListeners on this webpage. First is when the page laods to call a function that opens a dialog with information about cookies.

Second is when you click any of those 2 buttons to "accept" the cookies and the third one is when you press the Enter key, also for "accepting" the cookies and to close the dialog box.

Right after you close the dialog box regarding cookies, an animation that moves the main character is triggered via a function call.

Hovering the mouse over the turtle will trigger another EventListener to make the animal "speak".

Double-clicking on the character makes this one "jump" and thicking the box in the right section will change the color of the turtle.

There are also some EventListeners when some animations end to change some style properties for different elements.
